Information all beginning students need:

1) One important piece of information students need is to know their student ID. The Student ID is a unique code for each student. Students who have just arrived at school to confirm that they came to school at the beginning of the school day use this code for manual entry. When you have a special card you just need to swipe the card reader and you do not need to enter the code manually.
